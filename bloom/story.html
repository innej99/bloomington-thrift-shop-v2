The Bloomington Thrift Shop, Inc. is a not-for-profit resale shop. It operates as the fund-raising arm of the Zeta Chapter of Psi Iota Xi, a national philanthropic sorority. The shop has been in operation in Bloomington since 1936.

The proceeds from your purchases provide scholarships to members of the community as well as funding for over 50 community service programs in Monroe County and throughout Indiana.

Donations of gently used items, from chapter members and people in our community, supply our shop with quality trendy and professional items. It also serves the community by providing an outlet for recycling clothing and goods as well as offering low priced quality merchandise.

We gladly accept donations of gently used items.
